Did an experiment with @chilcote's outset. Put 10.10.4Upd in firstboot-packages. Needed 2 boots & 755 permissions, but it worked! #MacAdmins

@AnthonyReimer the first boot was manually kicked off then, to initialize the install?

— Joseph Chilcote (@chilcote) July 8, 2015

@chilcote Actually, both boots were manual. The steps:
Copy pkg
Manual Reboot (outset triggered)
Wait until log says it's done
Manual reboot
