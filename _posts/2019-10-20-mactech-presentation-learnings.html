Your Audience is Broader Than You Think

I generally poll the audience for each talk in some way to get a better feel for the crowd. Along those lines, the best talks I saw at the conference were those who had a clearly delineated topic that was not too broad (‚Äúsimple‚Äù is the wrong adjective here).
