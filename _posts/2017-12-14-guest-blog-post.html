--- layout: post title: Guest Blog Post at Amsys.uk ---

As you may have read online, I’m pleased to be speaking at the Mac Admin & Developers Conference UK (MacAD.UK) in London in February. Presumably as a way to promote the Conference, Amsys, the UK training company that organizes MacAD.UK, has asked speakers to write a blog post expanding a bit on what their session is going to be about. Since they already have an established audience for their blog, this should be a win-win. I’m very interested to see how this works out, in case that is something we might want to consider establishing for MacDeployment.

My post is the first off the mark. You can read it at http://www.amsys.co.uk/2017/12/deployment-need-talk/. There are some great people speaking at that conference. After what I wrote in my last blog post (“My name on the speaker roster is not going to sell a single ticket”), it’s ironic that my name shows up first on the speaker roster (alphabetically by first name). Keep scrolling, trust me — you’ll see some very fine European and North American speakers there.
