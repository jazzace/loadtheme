Anthony Reimer’s Blog for Mac Admins

This is my blog for Mac Admins, focusing on things that may be of interest to those who run Mac computer Labs like I do.

As of 2020, I have migrated all my presentation resources and blog-like posts that had appeared on the University of Calgary IAML web site to this personal site. The resource page is maclabs.jazzace.ca/resources/ and my blog-like posts have been posted here as if this blog existed when I wrote those posts. My Mac Hardware/Software Obsolescence Chart has also been relocated here (you can always go to obsolescence.jazzace.ca and be redirected to the right page).

For all my other personally-created content, go to my personal web site (Established 1996), also linked at the top of each page.
